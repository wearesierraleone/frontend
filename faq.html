Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the We Are Sierra Leone platform.

About the Platform

What is We Are Sierra Leone?

We Are Sierra Leone is a civic platform empowering citizens to voice their concerns anonymously, create petitions, and engage in meaningful dialogue about issues affecting Sierra Leone. Our goal is to create a safe space for open communication and civic engagement.

Who can use this platform?

Anyone concerned about civic issues in Sierra Leone can use this platform. Whether you're a citizen living in the country or a member of the diaspora, you're welcome to share your thoughts, experiences, and concerns.

Is the platform really anonymous?

Yes, all posts are anonymous by default. We don't collect personal information that can identify you. Your posts are not tied to your real identity, email address, or other personal information.

Using the Platform

How do I submit a post or report?

Click on the "Submit" link in the navigation menu. Fill out the form with your report details, select an appropriate category, and submit. Your post will be reviewed and published if it meets our community guidelines.

How do I create or sign a petition?

To create a petition, visit the "Petitions" page and click on "Start a Petition." Fill out the required information including the petition title, description, and target goal. To sign an existing petition, navigate to the petition page and click the "Sign this Petition" button.

Can I edit or delete my submissions?

Yes, you can edit or delete any post or petition you've created. Though the platform is anonymous, we use browser cookies to let you manage your own content. Look for the edit or delete options on content you've created.

Community Guidelines

Are there rules for posting?

While we encourage free expression, we have a zero-tolerance policy for hate speech, harassment, illegal content, or content that threatens harm to others. All posts should focus on civic issues and be presented in a constructive manner.

How can I report inappropriate content?

Each post has a "Report" button that you can use to flag content that violates our community guidelines. Our moderation team reviews all reports promptly and takes appropriate action.

How are petitions verified?

Our team reviews all petitions to ensure they address genuine civic concerns and comply with our community guidelines. We may request additional information or clarification from petition creators before publication.

Technical Questions

Is there a mobile app for the platform?

Currently, we offer a mobile-responsive web application that works well on smartphones and tablets. A dedicated mobile app is in our development roadmap for the future.

How can I improve my experience on slow internet connections?

Our platform is designed to work efficiently even with limited bandwidth. If you experience loading issues, try using the text-only version by adding "?lite=true" to the end of the URL.

How can I contact the team behind this platform?

You can reach us at support@wearesierraleone.org for any questions, concerns, or feedback.
