Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2025

At We Are Sierra Leone, we are committed to protecting your privacy while providing a platform for civic engagement. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We collect information you provide directly to us when using the platform:

  • Anonymous Identifier: When you interact with the platform, we may assign you an anonymous identifier to track your activities without revealing your identity.
  • Content: Information you post, such as petitions, comments, and votes.
  • Petition Signatures: When signing petitions, we collect your anonymous identifier and may optionally collect your name (if provided), location, and reason for signing.
  • Verification Information: When signing petitions, we ask you to confirm that you are a Sierra Leone citizen or resident.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you access or use our Service, we may automatically collect:

  • Usage Information: Information about your interactions with the Service, such as the pages or content you view and your actions on the Service.
  • Technical Information: Limited information from your device such as browser type and operating system to ensure the Service functions correctly.

We collect this information using browser storage and similar technologies. No personally identifiable information is stored permanently without your explicit consent.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Service.
  • Process and manage petition signatures and submissions.
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our Service.
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions, abuse, and other illegal activities.
  • Address problems with the Service.
  • Communicate with you about the Service, including sending updates about petitions you've signed or created.

3. Sharing of Information

3.1 With Your Consent

When you sign a petition, you consent to share your signature and any optional information provided (name, location, reason for signing) with:

  • Relevant government officials and departments to whom the petition is addressed.
  • Organizations working on the issue addressed in the petition.
  • Other users of the Service, in anonymized form.

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests from public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency).

4. Data Retention

We retain information as long as necessary to provide our Service and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. We may retain certain information for legitimate business purposes or as required by law.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the information we have about you.
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your information.
  • Object to processing of your information.
  • Request restriction of processing of your information.
  • Request transfer of your information.

Due to the anonymous nature of our platform, fulfilling some of these rights may require verification of your identity or anonymous identifier.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we become aware that a child under 16 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to remove such information.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, no internet or electronic transmission is ever fully secure or error-free.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will notify you by revising the date at the top of the policy and, in some cases, we may provide you with additional notice (such as adding a statement to our homepage or sending you a notification).

9. Specific Information for Petition Signers

When signing petitions on our platform:

  • Your signature count is visible to others to show petition support.
  • Your location (region only) may be used for demographic analysis.
  • If you provide your name, it will be visible alongside your signature.
  • Reasons for signing (if provided) may be shared with government officials and displayed on the petition page.
  • When a petition reaches its target or deadline, all signatures will be compiled and may be submitted to relevant government departments or officials.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
